About My Gear

I use gear that is simple but powerful.
I shoot all of my photos with an iPhone 14 Pro Max.

The portability and battery life of the iPhone allows me to easily bring my camera with me to all kinds of terrains and locations, while the camera and image processing on the iPhone allow the photos to still look amazing, despite the fact that they were taken on a mobile device.
